DataPlay - explore data in a playful way

DataPlay is a data analysis and exploratory game application which has been designed around users who may not possess expertise in data analysis or data mining, yet still have a requirement to extract meaningful stories from granular data, in order to bring that raw data to life.

Visual Data Exploration

Because data is easier to understand when it's visualised, users are presented with a number of different ways to view different types of data, be they singular, automatically correlated by the system or discovered by other users. Users can then start to look for patterns and correlations as they begin to play with the data.

Pattern discovery

Users are encouraged and rewarded for discovering previously unobserved datasets and any patterns within them. Multiple users can then collaborate to add further meaning to discovered patterns, validating or invalidating these discoveries, making observations, or searching for deeper connections - altering both their own reputation and the reputations of other users as they go.
